## 📖 What I Did

As part of a group, I contributed to both the system design and the implementation of the C program. The work required coordinating with team members, dividing tasks, and integrating individual contributions into a coherent final product. I also helped write and structure the project report.

---

## 💭 Reflection

**What I gained:** Experience working on a technical project in a team — dividing work, integrating code from multiple contributors, and producing a combined report. The project also deepened my understanding of COA concepts through applying them rather than just studying them.

**Suggestions for improvement:** More defined milestones and peer review checkpoints during the project would help teams catch integration issues earlier rather than at the final submission stage.

**Other thoughts:** Group projects in Year 1 taught me that technical ability is only part of the challenge. Communication, version control discipline, and clear task ownership matter just as much when multiple people are contributing to one codebase.
