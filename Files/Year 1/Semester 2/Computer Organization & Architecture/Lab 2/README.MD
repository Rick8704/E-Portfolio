## 📖 What I Did

I worked through arithmetic operations in binary, designed circuits that perform addition and subtraction, and documented the results. The exercises reinforced how the ALU (Arithmetic Logic Unit) carries out computation at the hardware level.

---

## 💭 Reflection

**What I gained:** A clearer understanding of how arithmetic is performed in hardware. Seeing binary addition done gate-by-gate made the relationship between logic circuits and computation concrete.

**Suggestions for improvement:** Extending the lab to include multiplication and division circuits would give a more complete picture of ALU operations.

**Other thoughts:** Lab 2 was the point where Year 1 Digital Logic and Year 1 Semester 2 COA came together. Recognising that connection made both subjects feel more cohesive.
