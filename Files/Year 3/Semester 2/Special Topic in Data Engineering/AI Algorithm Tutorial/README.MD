## 💭 Reflection

**What I gained:** A more critical perspective on AI model evaluation. Understanding that a model's performance depends on the metric chosen  and that accuracy alone is often insufficient  is an important point of practical wisdom for any data engineering work involving AI components.

**Suggestions for improvement:** Including a comparison of multiple model architectures on the same dataset would add depth. Seeing how different models perform differently on the same task and understanding why is more instructive than evaluating a single model in isolation.

**Other thoughts:** This tutorial reinforced a key principle: evaluation is not just a final step which is part of the design process. Choosing the right evaluation metric before training, not after, shapes the entire development approach and ensures the model is optimised for the right objective.
