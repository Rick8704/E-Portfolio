## 💭 Reflection

**What I gained:** A complete understanding of the end-to-end BI process — from raw data to business-facing insight — and the skills to deliver that process using Alteryx and Power BI. The project demonstrated that BI is as much about communication as it is about analysis.

**Suggestions for improvement:** Presenting to actual industry stakeholders — or at least a panel unfamiliar with the dataset — would create a more realistic presentation environment. Explaining findings to someone who does not know the data requires much clearer communication than presenting to a lecturer who does.

**Other thoughts:** Phase 3 brought closure to the most extended and structured project I had completed in Year 3. Looking back across all three phases, I can see how the careful work in Phase 1 — getting the data right — made the analysis and presentation in later phases possible. That lesson about the importance of foundational data quality is one I will carry into all future data engineering work.
