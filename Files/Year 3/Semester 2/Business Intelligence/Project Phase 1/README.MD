## 💭 Reflection

**What I gained:** Experience in the data preparation stage of a BI project — the part that often takes the most time but is least visible in the final output. Using Alteryx to handle data quality issues systematically was more efficient than manual cleaning in Python.

**Suggestions for improvement:** More guidance on how to select and frame a good business question — one that is specific enough to be answerable and meaningful enough to be useful — would strengthen the early stages of BI projects.

**Other thoughts:** Phase 1 taught me that good analysis starts with good questions and clean data. Rushing to visualise before the data is understood and prepared reliably leads to misleading outputs. The discipline of working through Phase 1 thoroughly made Phases 2 and 3 significantly smoother.
