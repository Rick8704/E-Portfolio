05 — Reflection

What I've learned

This semester has been among the most applied of my degree. Working through Special Topics in Data Engineering pushed me to connect the dots between tools I'd studied separately — Azure's cloud infrastructure, Spark's distributed processing, and AI workflows — and see how they form a coherent data engineering stack.

The PPG industry project was especially grounding. It's different working toward a real brief with real constraints, rather than a well-defined academic problem. I learned to communicate technical decisions more clearly and to scope work realistically.

If I could suggest one improvement: more structured exposure to data pipeline monitoring and observability earlier in the curriculum. I found myself piecing this together on the fly during the industry project, and earlier grounding would have made that smoother.
